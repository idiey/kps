# Workshop Management System: Value Evaluation & Pricing Proposal

## 🇲🇾 Malaysian Market Value Proposition

For a private workshop servicing government vehicles, this system is not just "software"—it is an **Operational Integrity Engine**. The value is derived from solving the specific pain points of **Business-to-Government (B2G)** interactions in Malaysia.

### 1. Guaranteed Compliance (Kepatuhan Borang)
- **KEW.PA-10 Automation:** The system digitizes the official government asset maintenance form. This ensures that every job submitted to the government follows the exact protocol required by the Treasury (Perbendaharaan).
- **Elimination of Errors:** Manual KEW.PA-10 forms are often rejected due to missing technical details or signatures. This system enforces data integrity at every step.

### 2. Accelerated Payment Reconciliation (Tuntutan Bayaran Pantas)
- **Evidence-Based Claims:** The integrated **Before/During/After photo documentation** provides undeniable proof of work. Government departments are much more likely to approve payments quickly when clear visual evidence is attached to the claim.
- **Parts & Cost Transparency:** The system tracks every nut and bolt with its associated cost, generating a clear breakdown that matches government procurement requirements.

### 3. Professionalism & Tender Competitive Advantage
- **Digital Branding:** When the workshop presents reports generated by this system, they stand out as a "Tier-1" modernized vendor.
- **Audit Readiness:** During a government audit (Surveilans), the workshop can instantly retrieve history for any vehicle No. Plat, showing who inspected it, what was replaced, and who approved it. This increases the workshop's "Trust Score" for future tender renewals.

### 4. Role-Based Accountability (Integritas Kerja)
- **Tiered Approval:** The system forces a separation of duties (Pentadbiran, Penyelia, Pemeriksa, Juruteknik). This prevents internal fraud and ensures quality control before the vehicle is returned to the government department.

## 💰 Recommended Pricing Model

Based on the current Malaysian IT procurement landscape for specialized B2G systems:

### Option 1: The "Digital Partner" (Subscription)

*Best for long-term partnership and recurring revenue.*
- **Setup & Onboarding:** RM 5,000 (One-time: Server setup, data migration, staff training).
- **Monthly Subscription:** RM 850 - RM 1,200 / month.
- **Annual Maintenance (AMC):** Included in subscription.

### Option 2: The "Outright Ownership" (Project-Based)

*Best if the client wants the system on their own server with no monthly fees.*
- **Total Project Cost:** **RM 35,000 - RM 55,000**.
- **Payment Milestone:** 30% Deposit, 40% Completion, 30% Handover.
- **Annual Maintenance (AMC):** RM 3,000 / year (after 1st year warranty).

### Option 3: Enterprise Customization (High Volume)

*If the workshop manages multiple branches or high vehicle volume (>500 jobs/month).*
- **Total Project Cost:** **RM 80,000 - RM 120,000**.
- **Includes:** Custom integration with government ERPs (if required), high-availability server setup, and dedicated on-site support.
